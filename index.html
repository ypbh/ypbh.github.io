Crazy Award

表彰自洽的疯狂 · Celebrating Self-Consistent Madness

第一章　总则

Chapter 1 · General Provisions

Crazy奖是一个娱乐性奖项。它表彰的不是“被验证的正确”，而是“自洽的疯狂”。它不依附于任何学术机构、任何科研基金、任何国家奖项。它的存在本身，就是对主流评价体系的一次穿孔。

The Crazy Award is an entertainment award. It honors not "verified correctness," but "self-consistent madness." It is not affiliated with any academic institution, research fund, or national prize. Its very existence is a perforation of the mainstream evaluation system.

第二章　得奖条件

Chapter 2 · Award Criteria

条件一：绝对自洽

Criterion 1 · Absolute Self-Consistency

理论内部不能有逻辑矛盾。从第一条公理到最后一个推论，必须形成完整的闭环。

The theory must contain no internal logical contradictions. From the first axiom to the final deduction, it must form a complete closed loop.

条件二：极度疯狂

Criterion 2 · Extreme Madness

理论的核心直觉，必须是主流学术界不敢碰、不愿碰、或者碰了就被边缘化的东西。

The core intuition of the theory must be something that mainstream academia dares not touch, refuses to touch, or has marginalized after touching.

条件三：直觉驱动

Criterion 3 · Intuition-Driven

理论的起点必须是一个或几个强烈的直觉，而不是数学推导。

The starting point of the theory must be one or several powerful intuitions, not mathematical derivations.

条件四：概念完整

Criterion 4 · Conceptual Completeness

理论必须是一个体系。有本体论，有操作论，有演化论，有伦理警告。

The theory must be a system. It must have ontology, operational theory, evolution theory, and ethical warnings.
